3、脚本：按设备名字找 IRQ，并将中断调度转移到cpu2
```cpp
#!/bin/bash

USB_IRQ=$(grep xhci-hcd /proc/interrupts | awk '{print $1}' | tr -d :)
//XDMA_IRQ=$(grep xdma /proc/interrupts | awk '{print $1}' | tr -d :)

echo 4 > /proc/irq/$USB_IRQ/smp_affinity
//echo 8 > /proc/irq/$XDMA_IRQ/smp_affinity
```
